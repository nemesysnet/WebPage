Why Choosing a Third Party Testing Team?

Visibility and correct coverage could be overlooked when testing is conducted in house. Depending on the scale and complexity of the project, independent testing can spot issues according with requirements that could be permeated by development and in house testing. An impartial, objective and experienced team will ensure the correct testing best practices are applied so releases can be delivered as smoothly and bug free as possible.

Gain Visibility

Visibility can be overlooked when the attention is driven only by development teams, an independent entity can focus on the correct coverage and adherence to requirements documentation.

Release On-Time

Releases can fall easily behind if testing is not conducted in a timely manner. Agility is the name of the game, and on time releases mean that the features are correctly tested and critical issues fixed.

Proper Maintenance

Creating new test cases, test plans and test runs, is a fundamental key to quality assurance, but proper review, analysis and transformation of the previous test interations is also very important. With that in mind, we can create better test sprints with increase coverage, automation and forecasting based on analitics.
